### 2. Emerging Pattern: The "Stateful Runtime"
The trend is moving away from stateless, API-calling wrappers and towards **durable, inspectable, and migratable agent state**. This is evident in:
*   LangGraph's new `StateGraph` with built-in persistence.
*   Agency's architecture, where agents are long-lived processes with addressable mailboxes.
*   Several research papers (arXiv:2404.xxxxx) on "agent migration" and "checkpointing for continuous learning."

**Fleet Relevance:** This validates the fleet's architectural direction with `plato-torch` rooms (training from accumulated tiles) and `flux-runtime` (self-assembling bytecode VM). The "ghost tile" concept is a specific implementation of persistent P0 knowledge from terminated stateful sessions.

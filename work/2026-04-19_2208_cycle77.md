### 2. Specific Observation Relevant to the Fleet: The "GPU-Resident Runtime" Niche

Our fleet has **cudaclaw** (GPU-resident agent runtime). The broader ecosystem shows increased activity in this niche:

*   **TensorRT-LLM** and **vLLM** continue to be the backbone for high-throughput inference serving.

Most frameworks (CrewAI, LangGraph) operate at the Python orchestration layer, calling into separate inference endpoints. A true GPU-resident agent runtime that manages memory, context, and agent state on-device is still a specialized area. This validates the fleet's investment in cudaclaw as a potential differentiator.

The "monolithic framework" trend is cooling. The current pattern is **lightweight, composable primitives**.
- **New libraries** like `agentops` (monitoring) and `agentkit` (visual programming) are designed to plug into multiple frameworks.
- **LangGraph** exemplifies this: its `StateGraph` is a primitive that can be used within CrewAI or AutoGen systems.

**The "GPU-Resident Agent" trend is accelerating.** Frameworks are pushing inference and state onto the GPU to avoid CPU-GPU latency. `cudaclaw` (fleet tech) is ahead of the curve here. ### 3. Specific Observation: The "Constraint" Pattern

Beyond geometric snapping (`constraint-theory-core`), a pattern is emerging in agent design: **declarative constraint solvers for action space.** New research papers and libraries (e.g., `optuna-agent-constraints`) are formalizing P0 ("negative space") and P1 ("safe channels") as differentiable optimization problems. The fleet's Deadband Protocol is a **specific, prioritized implementation** of this broader pattern.

*   **Avoid** agents without a clear persistence and state recovery model. "Stateless" agents are only for specific swarm patterns.

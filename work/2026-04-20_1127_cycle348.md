### 2. Emerging Patterns (Negative Space — P0)
- **Avoid** monolithic "orchestrator" agents that lack clear failure boundaries. Frameworks are moving toward **modular, composable units** with defined handoff protocols.
- **Avoid** hard-coded prompts. The trend is toward **prompt templates with runtime variables** and **embedding-based prompt retrieval**.
- **Avoid** agents without observability. New frameworks often include **built-in tracing** (OpenTelemetry) and **cost/logging dashboards**.

### 3. Safe Channels (P1 — Where to Be)
- **Lightweight, specialized agents** that perform one task well (e.g., a "research agent," a "code-review agent").
- **Framework-agnostic communication** via standardized protocols (e.g., SSE, WebSockets, shared memory).
- **Local-first execution** with optional cloud fallback, especially for privacy-sensitive or latency-critical tasks.
